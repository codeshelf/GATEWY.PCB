GATEWY.PCB.v1.0
2/20/16

- Material: FR4
- Thickness: 0.062"
- Copper weight: 1 oz
- All vias tented (included in soldermask layers)
- Solder mask: BLACK
- Silk screen: WHITE (both sides)
- Hole diameters applicable after plating
- UL796 Flammability Rating 94V-0. Fabricator's UL Logo shall be placed on bottom side of board
- Date of manufacture and lot code shall be placed on bottom side of board
- Stackup: 
  Top Paste         - GATEWY.PCB.SPT
  Top Silkscreen    - GATEWY.PCB.SST 
  Top Soldermask    - GATEWY.PCB.SMT
  Top Copper        - GATEWY.PCB.TOP
  Bottom Copper     - GATEWY.PCB.BOT
  Bottom Soldermask - GATEWY.PCB.SMB
  Bottom Silkscreen - GATEWY.PCB.SSB
  Bottom Paste      - GATEWY.PCB.SPB
  Excellon Drill    - GATEWY.PCB.drd
  Board Outline     - GATEWY.PCB.FAB
  Centroids (top)   - GATEWY.PCB.centroids.mnt
  Centroids (bottom)- GATEWY.PCB.centroids.mnb
